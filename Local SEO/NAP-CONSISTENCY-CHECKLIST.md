## 📝 NAP RULES (FOLLOW EXACTLY)

### Business Name:
✅ **Correct:** Instant Auto Traders
❌ **Wrong:** Instant Auto Traders Sydney
❌ **Wrong:** Instant Auto Traders - Cash for Cars
❌ **Wrong:** InstantAutoTraders (no space)

**Rule:** Use legal business name only, no keywords

### Address:
✅ **Correct:** 123 Main Street (spell out "Street")
❌ **Wrong:** 123 Main St (abbreviated)
❌ **Wrong:** 123 Main St. (with period)
❌ **Wrong:** Suite 5, 123 Main Street (unless this is your EXACT address)

**Rules:**
- Spell out Street, Road, Avenue, Boulevard (no abbreviations)
- No periods (St. → Street)
- Use exact address from Google Business Profile
- Include suite/unit number if applicable

### City:
✅ **Correct:** Sydney
❌ **Wrong:** Sydney, NSW
❌ **Wrong:** Sydney NSW 2000
❌ **Wrong:** sydney (lowercase)

**Rule:** City name only, proper capitalization

### State/Province:
✅ **Correct:** NSW
❌ **Wrong:** New South Wales (spell out)
❌ **Wrong:** nsw (lowercase)
❌ **Wrong:** N.S.W. (with periods)

**Rule:** Use standard abbreviation, uppercase

### Phone:
✅ **Correct:** +61 2 9XXX XXXX (international format)
✅ **Also Okay:** (02) 9XXX XXXX (Australian format)
❌ **Wrong:** 02 9XXX XXXX (no parentheses)
❌ **Wrong:** +61-2-9XXX-XXXX (dashes instead of spaces)
❌ **Wrong:** Different number on different sites

**Rule:** Pick ONE format and use it everywhere

### Website:
✅ **Correct:** https://instantautotraders.com.au
✅ **Also Okay:** https://www.instantautotraders.com.au
❌ **Wrong:** http://instantautotraders.com.au (no SSL)
❌ **Wrong:** instantautotraders.com.au (no protocol)
❌ **Wrong:** www.instantautotraders.com.au (missing https://)

**Rule:** Include https:// or https://www, be consistent

## 🛠️ HOW TO FIX INCONSISTENT NAP

### Step 1: Identify Inconsistencies (30 mins)

**Google yourself:**
```
"Instant Auto Traders" Sydney
```

Look at all results on first 3 pages. Note any inconsistencies:
- Different phone numbers
- Different addresses
- Different business names
- Abbreviated vs spelled-out street names

**Make a list of sites that need updating.**

### Step 2: Prioritize Fixes (10 mins)

**Fix in this order:**
1. Google Business Profile (MOST IMPORTANT)
2. Your website
3. Facebook, Bing, Apple Maps
4. Major directories (Yelp, True Local, Yellow Pages)
5. All other directories

### Step 3: Update One by One (Variable time)

For each inconsistent listing:
1. Log into the platform
2. Find "Edit business info"
3. Update to match your official NAP
4. Save changes
5. Mark as fixed in tracking sheet

**Time estimate:** 5-10 mins per listing

### Step 4: Remove Duplicate Listings (If Found)

**If you find duplicate Google Business Profiles:**
1. Claim both listings
2. Mark duplicate as "Closed"
3. Request removal via Google Business Profile support
4. Keep only ONE active listing

**Why this matters:** Duplicate listings confuse Google and hurt rankings.

## 🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING

**Problem:** I found 3 different phone numbers for my business online
**Solution:** Pick your current business phone as the official one, update all listings to match

**Problem:** Some directories won't let me edit my listing
**Solution:** Claim the listing first, then you can edit. If you can't claim it, contact directory support.

**Problem:** My old address is showing on some sites
**Solution:** Update Google Business Profile first (most important), then update all directories. Old address will gradually disappear from Google.

**Problem:** Different directories require different address formats
**Solution:** Adapt to their format (some require separate fields for street, city, state) but keep the core info identical

**Problem:** I have a P.O. Box and a physical address
**Solution:** Use physical address for Google Business Profile and local SEO. Only use P.O. Box for mail correspondence.
