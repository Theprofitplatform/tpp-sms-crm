## 🎁 INCENTIVE IDEAS (Optional)

**IMPORTANT:** You CANNOT offer payment for reviews (violates Google policy).

**Allowed:**
✅ "As a thank you, your next transaction gets $50 bonus" (for leaving honest feedback, not specifically 5-star)
✅ "We appreciate all reviews - enter to win $100 gift card" (drawing among all reviewers)
✅ "Donate $10 to charity for every review we receive"

**Not Allowed:**
❌ "$20 for a 5-star review"
❌ "Get paid to leave a review"
❌ "We'll give you $50 if you leave a positive review"

**My recommendation:** Don't offer incentives initially. Most happy customers will leave reviews if you make it easy. Save incentives for if you're struggling to get traction.

```
Thanks [Name]! We're thrilled you had a great experience selling your [Car Make]. Same-day service and fair prices are what we're all about. If you or anyone you know needs to sell a car in Sydney, we're always here to help! - [Your Name], Instant Auto Traders

**4-Star Review:**
```

**Detailed Positive Review:**
```

```

### Negative Review Response Templates

**1-2 Star Review (Legitimate Issue):**
```
Hi [Name], we're truly sorry you had this experience. This doesn't reflect our usual standards, and we take your feedback very seriously. Please call us at [Phone] so we can make this right. We want to turn this around for you. - [Your Name], Instant Auto Traders
```

**3-Star Review (Mixed Feedback):**
```
Thanks for your honest feedback, [Name]. We're glad [positive aspect they mentioned], but we're sorry about [negative aspect]. We're always working to improve, and your input helps us get better. If you'd like to discuss further, please reach out at [Phone]. - [Your Name]
```

**Fake/Spam Review:**
```
Hi [Name], we don't have any record of doing business with you. If this is an error, please contact us at [Phone] so we can clarify. If this review is intended for another business, we'd appreciate you updating it. Thanks - [Your Name], Instant Auto Traders
```

**Note:** You can also flag fake reviews to Google for removal.

## 🚫 REVIEW REQUEST DON'TS

❌ **Don't be pushy** - One follow-up max
❌ **Don't ask for "5-star reviews"** - Ask for "honest feedback"
❌ **Don't write fake reviews** - Google will catch you and penalize
❌ **Don't offer payment for reviews** - Violates Google policy
❌ **Don't cherry-pick reviewers** - Ask all customers, not just happy ones
❌ **Don't ignore negative reviews** - Respond professionally to all
❌ **Don't get into arguments** - Stay professional even if review is unfair
❌ **Don't wait too long** - Ask within 24-48 hours of transaction

---

## ✅ REVIEW REQUEST DO'S

✅ **Make it easy** - Direct link, not "search for us on Google"
✅ **Ask at the right time** - When customer is happy (during/right after sale)
✅ **Personalize** - Use their name and mention their specific car
✅ **Follow up once** - One reminder is okay if they don't respond
✅ **Respond to all reviews** - Within 24 hours
✅ **Thank reviewers** - Show appreciation
✅ **Be genuine** - Authentic requests get better responses
✅ **Track systematically** - Use spreadsheet to stay organized
