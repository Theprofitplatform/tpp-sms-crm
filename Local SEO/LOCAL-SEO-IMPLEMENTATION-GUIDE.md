## ⭐ PHASE 2: GET YOUR FIRST 5 REVIEWS

**Goal:** 5 reviews with 4.5+ star average within 1 week

### Why Reviews Matter:
- **Local ranking factor** (top 3 most important)
- **Trust signal** (79% of consumers trust online reviews)
- **Click-through rate** (businesses with 5+ reviews get 2x more clicks)

### Step 1: Get Your Review Link (5 mins)

1. Go to your Google Business Profile dashboard
2. Click "Get more reviews"
3. Copy your unique review link
4. It looks like: `https://g.page/r/[YOUR-ID]/review`

**Save this link** - you'll use it in emails and texts.

### Step 2: Identify Past Happy Customers (15 mins)

Look through your records for:
- Recent customers (last 3-6 months)
- Customers who left positive feedback
- Customers you had good rapport with
- Customers who paid fair prices

**Make a list of 10-15 customers** (you'll ask 5 at a time).

### Step 3: Send Review Requests (Ongoing)

**IMPORTANT:**
- ✅ DO: Ask customers who had positive experiences
- ✅ DO: Make it easy (direct link)
- ✅ DO: Follow up once if no response
- ❌ DON'T: Offer payment for reviews (violates Google policy)
- ❌ DON'T: Write fake reviews
- ❌ DON'T: Ask for only 5-star reviews (ask for "honest feedback")

## ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

❌ **Inconsistent NAP** - Biggest mistake! Use exact same format everywhere
❌ **Fake reviews** - Google will penalize you, not worth it
❌ **Wrong business category** - Hurts relevance
❌ **No photos** - Profiles with photos get 2x more engagement
❌ **Not responding to reviews** - Makes you look unprofessional
❌ **P.O. Box address** - Use real physical address
❌ **Keyword stuffing in business name** - "Best Cash Car Buyer Sydney #1" will get you penalized
❌ **Duplicate listings** - Confuses Google, hurts rankings
