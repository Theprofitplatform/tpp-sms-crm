🛞 Wheel Repairs

Can bent alloy wheels be repaired?

In most cases, yes! We use professional wheel straightening equipment to repair bent alloy wheels. However, severely damaged wheels may need replacement for safety reasons.

What is alloy wheel refurbishment?

Alloy wheel refurbishment involves restoring damaged wheels to like-new condition through processes including straightening, welding cracks, removing scratches, repainting, and powder coating.

How do I know if my wheel can be repaired or needs replacement?

Our expert technicians will assess your wheel damage and provide honest advice. We'll only recommend replacement if repair would compromise safety or structural integrity.

Do you repair cracked wheels?

Yes, we can repair certain types of cracks using specialized welding techniques. However, cracks in critical stress areas may require wheel replacement for safety.

🚗 Tyre Services

Can all tyre punctures be repaired?

Most punctures in the tread area can be repaired if they're less than 6mm in diameter. Sidewall damage, large punctures, or multiple punctures typically require tyre replacement.

What is wheel alignment and why is it important?

Wheel alignment ensures your wheels are set to the manufacturer's specifications. Proper alignment improves tyre life, fuel efficiency, and vehicle handling while preventing uneven wear.

How often should I get my tyres balanced?

We recommend tyre balancing every 10,000-15,000 km, when you rotate your tyres, or if you notice vibration while driving.

Do you sell new tyres as well as repair them?

Yes! We stock a wide range of quality tyres from leading brands. If repair isn't possible, we can supply and fit new tyres at competitive prices.
