---

### 5. **"Both" Device Tracking Doubles Your Keyword Count**

**The Problem:**
The CSV says `Device: both` for every keyword

**What This Actually Means:**
- SEMrush will track BOTH desktop AND mobile
- That's 100 keywords × 2 = **200 tracking credits**
- Your plan might exceed your SEMrush limit
- Desktop vs mobile rankings can differ by 10+ positions

**Better Approach:**
- Track mobile ONLY for local terms (car buyers near me)
- Track desktop for informational terms
- This saves 50 tracking credits
- Focus budget on what matters

**Grade: C (inefficient resource use)**

---

### 6. **No Search Intent Analysis**

**The Problem:**
Some keywords have WRONG intent for your business:

**Examples:**
- "buy my car" → User wants TO SELL (correct ✅)
- "buy used cars sydney" → User wants TO BUY FROM YOU (wrong ❌)
- "car buyers online" → Ambiguous intent
- "buyers of cars" → Who is this? Dealers? Individuals?

**What This Means:**
- You'll rank for keywords that don't convert
- Wasted traffic from wrong-intent keywords
- Confused users who bounce immediately
- Wrong message on your pages

**Grade: D (intent mismatch will hurt conversions)**

---

### 7. **Location Tags are Inconsistent**

**The Problem:**
```csv
Location: Sydney NSW
Location: Parramatta NSW
Location: Newcastle NSW
```

**But:**
- Parramatta IS IN Sydney
- SEMrush location targeting should be "Sydney, NSW"
- Then track "car buyers parramatta" as keyword
- Not separate location tracking

**Why This Matters:**
- You're splitting your tracking incorrectly
- Should all be "Sydney, NSW" location
- Use the keyword itself for suburb targeting
- Current setup will give wonky data

**Grade: D (technical setup issue)**
