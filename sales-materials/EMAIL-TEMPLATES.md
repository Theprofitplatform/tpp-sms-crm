## CUSTOMIZATION NOTES

**Personalization Tokens:**
- [First Name] - Client's first name
- [Company Name] - Client's company
- [Website] - Client's website URL
- [X] - Specific numbers from audit
- [DATE] - Current date
- [TIME] - Scheduled time
- [CALENDLY_LINK] - Your scheduling link

**Best Practices:**
1. Keep subject lines under 50 characters
2. Personalize beyond just the name
3. Include specific data from their audit
4. One clear call-to-action per email
5. Follow up 3 times max (3-day, 7-day intervals)
6. A/B test subject lines
7. Send during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
8. Tuesday-Thursday get best response rates
